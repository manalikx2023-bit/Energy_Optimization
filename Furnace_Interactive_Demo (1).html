How the AI Optimisation Model Reduces Energy Consumption
The model uses three calibrated regression engines trained on 30,731 slab records and 4,344 hours of process data to predict FCE dropout temperature, fuel consumption, and flue gas temperature — then recommends optimal zone setpoints that keep FCE ≥ Recipe Target while minimising total fuel.

ENERGY FORMULA — NET USABLE ENERGY CALCULATION
Energy = Total Fuel − ((COREX ÷ 5.12) + NG) Where: COREX ÷ 5.12 = calorific value adjustment for COREX gas (lower heating value factor) NG = natural gas volume (directly subtracted as it has higher energy density) Total Fuel = COREX + NG (Nm³/hr) Result: Net energy available for heating slabs after accounting for combustion inefficiencies
